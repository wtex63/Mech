# Electrical Wiring Review Checklist

## Safety

- [ ] Voltage and current ratings are specified.
- [ ] Protection elements (fuse, TVS, breaker) are documented.
- [ ] Grounding strategy is defined.

## Connectivity

- [ ] Every connector pin is labeled.
- [ ] Wire colors and gauges are listed.
- [ ] Polarity and orientation are unambiguous.

## Documentation Quality

- [ ] Diagram revision is shown.
- [ ] BOM references are consistent with diagram labels.
- [ ] PDF export is legible at print scale.
